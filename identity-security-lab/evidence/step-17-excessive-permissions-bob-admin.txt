Step 17 Evidence: Excessive Permissions Simulation

User:
- bob

Intended Role:
- app-manager

Excessive Role Added:
- app-admin

Observed Result:
- Manager Area: Allowed
- Admin Area: Allowed

Security Finding:
Bob was able to access admin-level functionality because he had an excessive role assignment.

Security Lesson:
Identity systems require periodic access reviews to detect and remove unnecessary privileges.
