Step 19 Evidence: Privilege Escalation Through Composite Role

User:
- alice

Intended Role:
- app-user

Misconfiguration:
- app-user was configured to include app-admin.

Observed Result:
- User Area: Allowed
- Admin Area: Allowed

Security Finding:
Alice gained admin access through role inheritance even though she was only intended to be a standard user.

Security Lesson:
Composite roles and inherited permissions must be reviewed carefully because low-privilege roles can accidentally inherit high-privilege access.
